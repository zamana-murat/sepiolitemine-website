## The World's Most Effective Natural Cat Litter Mineral

Sepiolite has revolutionised the pet care industry, becoming the preferred mineral for premium cat litter worldwide. Its exceptional microporous structure absorbs up to 150% of its weight in moisture while neutralising ammonia and bacterial odours naturally — without chemicals or fragrances.

Each sepiolite granule contains millions of microscopic channels that trap and lock moisture instantly, significantly outperforming traditional bentonite clay and keeping the litter box drier for longer periods between changes. Unlike chemical deodorizers that mask smells, sepiolite physically adsorbs ammonia and volatile organic compounds within its pore network, delivering genuine odour elimination that lasts days rather than hours.

Sepiolite litter weighs approximately 40% less than equivalent bentonite products — a major competitive advantage on retail shelves where convenience drives purchasing decisions. Our proprietary screening process eliminates fine particles, producing a virtually dust-free litter that also minimises tracking outside the box. We supply both clumping and non-clumping formulations to suit every consumer preference.

For brands and retailers, we offer comprehensive white-label and private-label programmes with custom granule sizes (0–2 mm, 2–4 mm, 4–8 mm), scented and unscented options, and packaging from 5L bags to 20L boxes. We currently supply sepiolite cat litter to leading brands across Europe, the Middle East, and North Africa, with FOB shipments from Mersin or Samsun Port.
