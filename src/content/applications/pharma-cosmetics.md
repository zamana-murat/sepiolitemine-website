## GRAS-Listed Mineral for Healthcare & Beauty

Sepiolite holds GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) status and is used across the pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries as an excipient, thickener, active ingredient carrier, and functional filler. Its biocompatibility, high surface area, and natural origin make it ideal for health-conscious formulations — from controlled-release tablets to luxury facial masks to natural deodorants.

In pharmaceutical tablets, sepiolite serves as a disintegrant, filler, and flow agent. Its high porosity enables rapid water uptake and tablet break-up, while its excellent flow properties improve die filling in high-speed tablet presses. The channel structure can also be loaded with active pharmaceutical ingredients for slow, sustained release over 6–24 hours — reducing dosing frequency and improving patient compliance for chronic medications.

In cosmetics, sepiolite provides shear-thinning rheology that feels luxurious in creams, lotions, and emulsions. It stabilises oil-in-water and water-in-oil emulsions without synthetic polymers, supporting clean beauty positioning. Its natural adsorption draws out excess sebum and unclogs pores in facial masks, and its odour-adsorbing properties make it an effective base for aluminium-free natural deodorants.

For veterinary and human gastro-intestinal applications, sepiolite binds aflatoxins, ochratoxins, and bacterial endotoxins in the gut, preventing absorption. We provide full regulatory documentation including Certificates of Analysis per batch, MSDS, technical dossiers, and DMF access for pharmaceutical customers across the EU, US, and global markets.
