## Engineered Granules for Maximum Performance

Our granulated sepiolite is produced by crushing and screening raw ore into precisely controlled size fractions. The granulation process preserves the mineral's natural fibrous microstructure while creating uniform, free-flowing granules with exceptional absorption capacity.

Available in three standard size fractions — 0–2mm (fine), 2–4mm (medium), and 4–8mm (coarse) — each optimized for specific end-use applications. Custom size fractions can be produced upon request for clients with specialized requirements.

The low-dust formulation makes our granulated sepiolite particularly valued in the cat litter industry, where clean handling and superior odor control are essential. Agricultural clients benefit from the granules' slow-release carrier properties for fertilizers and pesticides.

Each batch undergoes rigorous quality control including sieve analysis, moisture testing, and absorption capacity measurement. Consistent quality across shipments ensures your production line runs without interruption.
