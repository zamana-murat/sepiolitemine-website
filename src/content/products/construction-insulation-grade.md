## Building Better with Natural Mineral Fiber

Construction and insulation grade sepiolite leverages the mineral's natural fibrous structure and exceptionally low thermal conductivity to enhance a wide range of building materials. When incorporated into cement, plaster, and panel formulations, sepiolite fibers act as natural micro-reinforcement.

The mineral's inherent fire resistance — it is classified as non-combustible (Euroclass A1) — makes it invaluable in fireproof coatings, fire doors, and passive fire protection systems. Sepiolite maintains its structural integrity at temperatures exceeding 800°C.

In thermal insulation applications, sepiolite-based boards and panels deliver low-lambda performance while remaining lightweight and easy to install. The mineral's natural porosity traps air within its fiber structure, creating an effective thermal barrier.

Our construction grade is produced in both powder and short-fiber forms to suit different incorporation methods. We work closely with building material manufacturers to optimize grade specifications for their specific formulation requirements.
