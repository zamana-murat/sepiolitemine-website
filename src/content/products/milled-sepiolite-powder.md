## Precision-Milled for Advanced Applications

Our milled sepiolite powder is produced through a controlled grinding and classification process that reduces raw ore to ultra-fine particles while preserving the mineral's unique fibrous nanostructure. This structure is key to its exceptional rheological and adsorptive properties.

Available in multiple grades from 5 to 200 microns, the powder exhibits remarkably high specific surface area (200–300 m²/g) that provides outstanding thixotropic behavior in liquid systems. This makes it indispensable as a rheology modifier in paints, coatings, and adhesives.

In pharmaceutical and cosmetic applications, milled sepiolite serves as an excipient, carrier, and stabilizer. Its natural origin, chemical inertness, and high purity make it suitable for sensitive formulations where synthetic alternatives are undesirable.

Our milling facility operates with precision air classifiers that ensure tight particle size distribution. D50 and D90 values are verified on every batch, giving formulators the consistency they need for reproducible end products.
