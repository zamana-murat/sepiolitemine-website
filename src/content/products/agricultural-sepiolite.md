## The Natural Solution for Sustainable Agriculture

Agricultural sepiolite is a specially graded product designed for the farming and agrochemical industries. Its unique combination of high absorption capacity, controlled porosity, and chemical inertness makes it an ideal carrier substrate for fertilizers, herbicides, pesticides, and seed coatings.

When used as a soil amendment, sepiolite significantly improves soil structure in sandy and clay-heavy soils alike. Its porous fiber structure increases water retention capacity by up to 30%, reducing irrigation frequency and improving drought resilience for crops.

As a fertilizer carrier, sepiolite enables slow-release nutrient delivery. Active ingredients are adsorbed into the mineral's micropore network and released gradually over weeks, improving nutrient efficiency and reducing the environmental impact of agricultural runoff.

Our agricultural grade meets the quality standards required by major agrochemical manufacturers and is certified for organic farming applications in the European Union. We supply to clients across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.
