## Nature's Most Absorbent Mineral, Straight from the Earth

Raw sepiolite ore is a hydrated magnesium silicate clay mineral with a unique fibrous microstructure that gives it extraordinary absorptive and adsorptive properties. Our raw ore is extracted from open-pit mines in Türkiye's Eskişehir and Sivrihisar regions, home to the world's largest and highest-quality sepiolite reserves.

Unlike processed grades, raw sepiolite ore retains its full natural fiber length and crystalline integrity. This makes it the preferred starting material for clients who operate their own grinding, granulation, or calcination facilities and need maximum control over their end product's characteristics.

Each shipment is accompanied by a detailed Certificate of Analysis covering chemical composition, moisture content, and particle size distribution. We work closely with buyers to ensure the raw material meets their specific processing and quality requirements.

The mineral's natural porosity, with channels measuring approximately 3.7 x 10.6 angstroms, enables remarkable absorption capacity. Raw sepiolite ore can absorb up to 2.5 times its weight in liquid, making it one of the most efficient natural sorbents available in the industrial mineral market.
